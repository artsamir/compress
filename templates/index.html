Why You Should Compress Your Images

Large image files can slow down your projects and websites. When you compress images, you reduce their file size without losing important visual information. This is especially useful for presentations, assignments, and online portfolios. Smaller files load faster and take less storage space on your devices.

Technology

Understanding File Formats and When to Use Them

Different file formats serve different purposes. JPG files are good for photographs because they compress well. PNG files are better for graphics with transparent areas. PDF files are universal - they maintain their appearance on any device. Understanding which format to use for each situation helps you make better choices when working on projects or creating content for social media.
