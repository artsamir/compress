Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom tool development take?

+

Development timeline depends on project complexity. Simple tools can take 1-2 weeks, while complex projects may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

What AI tools do your prompts work with?

+

Our prompts are compatible with: DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, and most other modern AI image generators. Video prompts work with Runway, Synthesia, and similar platforms.

Can I use your free tools commercially?

+

Yes! All images processed with our free tools can be used for commercial purposes. You can use them in projects, sell them, or redistribute them without restrictions.

Do you offer refunds for custom tools?

+

Refunds are available during development with decreasing percentages based on completion. See our Refund Policy for complete details.

How can I integrate Cutcompress tools into my application?

+

We offer API access for integration. Contact us to discuss your integration requirements, and we'll provide documentation and support.
