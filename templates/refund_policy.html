Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Free Tools

All free tools on Cutcompress are provided at no cost. No refunds are applicable to free services.

2. Custom Tool Development

2.1 Development Phase Refunds

  • Before Development Starts: Full refund of deposit if cancelled within 24 hours
  • During Development (0-25% completion): 75% refund of total project cost
  • During Development (25-50% completion): 50% refund of total project cost
  • During Development (50-75% completion): 25% refund of total project cost
  • After 75% Completion: No refund, project must be completed

2.2 Post-Delivery Refunds

Once a custom tool is delivered and accepted:

  • 7-day warranty for critical bugs
  • Bug fixes and minor adjustments are provided free of charge
  • No refunds for change requests after delivery
  • Additional features requested after delivery are billed separately

2.3 Refund Conditions

Refunds will be processed only if:

  • The project cannot be completed as specified
  • Critical bugs prevent tool functionality
  • Poor communication or delays are due to our team

2.4 Refund Processing

  • Refund requests must be submitted in writing
  • Refunds are processed within 14 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited
  • Processing fees (if any) may apply

3. AI Prompts & Digital Products

3.1 Prompt Refund Policy

AI prompts are digital products with immediate delivery. Due to their nature:

  • No Refunds: Prompts cannot be refunded once purchased
  • Quality Guarantee: All prompts are tested before sale
  • Support: We provide free guidance on using your purchased prompts

3.2 Exceptions

Refunds may be issued if:

  • The prompt file is corrupted or cannot be accessed
  • The prompt does not match the product description
  • Payment processing error on our side

3.3 How to Request a Prompt Refund

  • Contact support with your order number
  • Describe the issue or reason for refund
  • Provide proof of purchase
  • We will respond within 48 hours

4. Payment Processing Issues

4.1 Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice for the same order:

  • Contact us immediately with transaction details
  • We will investigate and refund duplicate charges
  • Refunds typically processed within 5-7 business days

4.2 Unauthorized Transactions

If you did not authorize a transaction:

  • Report the issue within 30 days
  • We will investigate fraud claims
  • Full refund will be issued if fraud is confirmed

5. Refund Method & Timeline

  • Method: Refunds are credited to the original payment method
  • Processing Time: 5-14 business days depending on your bank
  • Bank Processing: Your bank may take additional time to reflect the credit
  • Contact: For refund status, contact our support team

6. Non-Refundable Items

  • Free tools and services
  • Digital prompts (except for technical issues)
  • Setup or consultation fees (after service provided)
  • Renewal or subscription-based services (unless stated otherwise)

7. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about our refund policy, please contact us.
