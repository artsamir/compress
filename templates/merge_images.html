📸 Tips & Tricks for Perfect Passport Photos

1️⃣ Why Background Matters

A clean background 🎨 makes your photo professional and acceptable for IDs & passports. With Cutcompress, you can remove backgrounds automatically and even add custom ones 🌆.

2️⃣ Choosing the Right Size

Always select the correct dimensions 📏 before merging. For example, 35x45 mm is the standard passport size 🛂 in many countries, while 20x25 mm works well for ID cards.

3️⃣ Smart Positioning

Use our drag-and-drop & resize ✋ feature to adjust photos perfectly. Keep the face centered 👤 and ensure equal margins for a natural look.

4️⃣ Pro Editing Hacks

➡ Use transparent backgrounds when unsure. ➡ Add white or light blue 🌤️ for official documents. ➡ Save in PNG for best quality 📂.

🌍 Printing & Sharing

8️⃣ Home Printing Setup

Use high-quality photo paper 🖼️ and set your printer to 300 DPI for sharp results. Choose matte or glossy finish depending on your preference ✨.

9️⃣ Printing at Shops

Export your photo in PDF or JPG 📂 and carry it to a nearby print shop. Always ask for photo-grade paper for long-lasting durability 📌.
